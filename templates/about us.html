About Us

Some information about our shop.

  • Step 1

    Login Page

    The login page is the gateway for users to access their accounts securely. Users can sign in using their credentials or create a new account.

  • Step 2

    Index Page

    The index page serves as the homepage, showcasing featured products, promotions, and navigation options for exploring the website.

  • Step 3

    Product Page

    The product page provides detailed information about individual items, including images, descriptions, pricing, and an option to add to the cart.

  • Step 4

    Cart Page

    The cart page displays items selected by the user. Customers can review their order, adjust quantities, or proceed to checkout.

  • Step 5

    Checkout Page

    The checkout page allows users to review their order, provide shipping and payment details, and finalize their purchase.
